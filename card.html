Chameleon

Their skin has two layers of nanocrystals that allow them to change color

  • Scientific Name: Chamaeleonidae
  • Average Length: 30 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Type: Reptile

They are animals that are characterized by the mobility independent of their eyes, their protractile tongue that allows them to catch their prey at a distance, the fingers grouped in two opposable blocks ensuring good grip on the branches and their ability to change color and finally their tail that serves to stabilize.
